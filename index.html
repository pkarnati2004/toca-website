Why we're different

Accessible pricing

With our unique mobile-based work platform, workers earn more and clients pay less – about 20% less than competitors such as Amazon Mechanical Turk.

Guaranteed high quality work

We guarantee 95% accuracy in our data annotation, achieved through cross validation by experts along with a unique work distribution model. Our proprietary talent identification model uses machine learning to find the most reliable workers, while our work distribution pipeline minimizes the correlation between errors, allowing us to correct any mis-annotations before they are finalized.

Ease of integration

A simple API that can easily integrate to your work process to send us your training data. We also offer an optional dedicated project manager.
